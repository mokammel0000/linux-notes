Yum: 
    Stands for Yellowdog Updater Modified.

    A command-line package management utility for RPM (Red Hat Package Manager) systems.

    Used in RPM-based Linux distributions, such as Fedora and Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL)

    Yum was developed by the Linux community 
        (to simplify) the process of installing, updating, and removing packages on RPM-based Linux distributions.
